About Us

PunkyCuts is a small local business creating prints, shirts, bags, mugs and more.

Residing in the Northside/Cincinnati area, all products are handmade out of her own home.

Exploring her love for creating and sharing handmade gifts, creator and owner Cassy ("Punky") Kohls started PunkyCuts upon receiving a Cricut for her birthday.
